### 1b. Scope Classification

### 1c. Explore and Interview

First, I need to understand where exactly the delay occurs. According to the protocol for performance issues, I must ask:

**Is the 3-second delay happening on:**
1. **Page load** - the entire page takes 3 seconds to appear
2. **Rendering after load** - page loads but form renders slowly
3. **User interaction** - form is visible but interactions are laggy
4. **API/form submission** - delay when submitting the form

This distinction is critical because each has completely different root causes:
- Page load → bundle size, server response, initial data fetching
- Rendering → hydration issues, heavy components, client-side data processing
- Interaction → excessive re-renders, unoptimized event handlers
- Submission → slow API endpoint, database query, validation logic

**Additional questions:**
1. Which registration form specifically? (`/register`, `/signup`, or another path?)
2. Are there any console errors or warnings during the slow load?
3. Does this happen in both development and production, or just one environment?

I'll wait for your answers before proceeding with the plan.
